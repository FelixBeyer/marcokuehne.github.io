About the author

 

Welcome! My name is Marco Kühne. The very first thing I want to do is to invite you to call me Marco. That is, if we meet on the street, you come talk to me during office hours, you ask some question; Marco’s the name that I respond to.

  • Web: http://marco-kuehne.com/
  • Twitter: https://twitter.com/marco_kuhne
  • GitHub: https://github.com/MarcoKuehne

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at the European University Viadrina (https://www.wiwi.europa-uni.de/). I am generally keen on teaching topics related to research design, quantitative methods, and statistical software. My main methodological interests in quantitative social science include panel data modelling, causal inference with observational data and R programming.

I am also a gardening fanatic, a coffee enthusiast, a committed ballroom and Discofox dancer, a (vegetarian) food lover. I enjoy cutting down big trees and practicing new languages in its own sake. Feel free to contact me!
