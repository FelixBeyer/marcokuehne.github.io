Aim of this books

I welcome you to join me on the way to become fluent in data.

The ability to take data — to be able to understand it, to process it, to extract value from it, to visualize it, to communicate it — that’s going to be a hugely important skill in the next decades…

Hal Varian, 2009. Googles chief economist. In: McKinsey Quarterly 2009

In addition, let’s foster people’s ability to replicate and validate the data work of others.

Data literacy is the ability to read, understand, create, and communicate data as information.
